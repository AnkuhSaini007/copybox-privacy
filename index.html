Privacy Policy for CopyBox

Last Updated: July 20, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how CopyBox ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Locally Stored Data

  • Text snippets and templates you create
  • Folder structures and organizational preferences
  • Local application settings (theme, shortcuts, etc.)

All locally stored data remains on your device only.

2.2 Google Drive Backup Data

If you choose to enable Google Drive backup:

  • Your snippets are encrypted and synced to your own Google Drive account
  • We only access the specific folder created by CopyBox in your Drive
  • We cannot access any other files in your Google Drive

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core extension functionality (storing and retrieving your snippets)
  • To sync your data across devices via your own Google Drive (only if you opt in)
  • To improve user experience (anonymous usage statistics only)

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties. Your data is stored:

  • Locally on your device (primary storage)
  • In your personal Google Drive (only if backup is enabled by you)

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access all your data stored by the extension
  • Delete your data at any time via the extension's Trash/Delete features
  • Revoke Google Drive access via your Google Account settings
  • Export your data from the extension settings

6. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data. All communications with Google Drive use secure HTTPS connections.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
